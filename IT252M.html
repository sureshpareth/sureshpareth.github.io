🚀 Major Projects

🏢 Office Automation System
Duration: 4 weeks (Weeks 4-7)

Objective: Design and implement a comprehensive office automation database system that manages employee information, project assignments, resource allocation, and workflow processes.

Key Features:
• Employee management with hierarchical reporting
• Project tracking and resource allocation
• Document management system
• Meeting scheduling and room booking
• Payroll and attendance management
• Report generation with forms and queries

Deliverables: ER diagram, normalized database schema, SQL implementation, forms, reports, and system documentation.
🏨 Hotel Management System
Duration: 4 weeks (Weeks 8-11)

Objective: Develop a complete hotel management database system that handles reservations, guest services, room management, and billing operations.

Key Features:
• Room inventory and availability management
• Guest reservation and check-in/check-out
• Service ordering and billing
• Staff management and scheduling
• Housekeeping and maintenance tracking
• Financial reporting and analytics

Deliverables: Complete database design, stored procedures for business logic, user interface forms, comprehensive reports, and testing documentation.
🏥 Hospital Management System
Duration: 4 weeks (Weeks 12-15)

Objective: Create a sophisticated hospital management database system that manages patient care, medical records, staff coordination, and administrative operations.

Key Features:
• Patient registration and medical history
• Doctor scheduling and appointment management
• Medical procedure and treatment tracking
• Pharmacy and inventory management
• Billing and insurance processing
• Emergency department workflow
• Medical equipment and facility management

Deliverables: Complete system analysis, database implementation, security features, backup/recovery procedures, and comprehensive system documentation.

📋 Project Requirements

Technical Requirements:

  • Complete ER modeling and normalization

  • Forms and reports development

Documentation:

  • System requirements analysis
