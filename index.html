About Me

With a rich experience spanning 22 years, I am a technology-driven professional, out of which approximately 17 years have been dedicated to teaching diverse computer science subjects. My career has been marked by significant achievements, including contributions to the design and development of critical systems such as Debenture Floatation, Hall Ticket & Rank Generation, Seat Allotment, and Centralized Admission for B.Ed & DEd programs. I possess a strong foundation in analytical thinking, adept problem-solving skills, and well-honed administrative capabilities.

🎓 Academic Leadership

Served as Principal at multiple institutions. Championed governance, curriculum innovation, and student mentoring systems.

🤖 AI/ML Expertise

Personally coordinated design and framing of syllabus for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in BTech programs.

🔬 Research Focus

Published in journals including Elsevier and De Gruyter on inverse problems, regularization, and neural network optimization.

🏗️ Systems Development

Designed critical academic systems including centralized admission, rank generation, and banking modules.
